# Important Dates

Track birthdays, anniversaries, and other milestones for people.

## Add a date

On a person's detail page, use the **Important Dates** section. Date of birth is read-only (set from the person's profile). Custom dates (anniversaries, etc.) can be added, edited, and deleted inline.

## On This Day

The dashboard shows an **Upcoming Moments** widget listing important dates in the next 30 days so you never miss a birthday or anniversary.
